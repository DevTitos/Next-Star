What is Celestial Chess?

Celestial Chess is an advanced strategy game that combines traditional chess concepts with mathematical principles and alien quantum technology. Two commanders battle across an 8x8 quantum matrix using mathematically-enhanced pieces.

Each player starts with 100 Quantum Energy that regenerates 10 points per turn. Use energy for special abilities:

  • Cooldown: 3 turns
  • Best with: Prime numbers

  2. Move Reversal: Access Temporal Loop
